Haochen Sun

I am a PhD candidate in the Data Systems Group at the Cheriton School of Computer Science, University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. Xi He. My research focuses on enhancing the security and privacy of machine learning and data management systems using differential privacy (DP) and cryptography. I am actively seeking a research internship in areas related to differential privacy, including secure computations for DP and DP foundation models.
