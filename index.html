Haochen Sun

I am a PhD student in the Data Systems Group at the Cheriton School of Computer Science, University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. Xi He. My research focuses on enhancing the security and privacy of machine learning and data management systems using differential privacy (DP) and cryptography.
