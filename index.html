Haochen Sun

I am a PhD student at the Data Systems Group within the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, supervised by Prof. Xi He. My current research focuses on enhancing the security and privacy of machine learning and data management systems using differential privacy (DP) and cryptography.
